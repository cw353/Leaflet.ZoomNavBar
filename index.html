L.ZoomNavBar Demo

L.ZoomNavBar is a simple zoom and navigation plugin for Leaflet. It combines zoom control and view history navigation into a single, compact, easy-to-use toolbar.

  • Click on the and buttons to zoom the map view in and out.
  • Click on the and buttons to navigate through map view history.
  • Click on the button to return to the initial map view.

You can choose to enable or disable the zoom buttons on the control, as shown in the examples below.
